Yes, we accept pets! All small, medium and large animals with the guide accessories used in the external areas of the pousada are welcome, however, only in the chalets, apartments do not have structure to accommodate pets.

We have apartments and chalets that accommodate up to 6 people, the maximum limit is this amount of people, remembering that the value of the chalet and apartment is always calculated by the amount of guests who will be installed, ie, if the rented apartment is for 6 people the value will be different than that rented for 5 people, so successively, the calculation is given: (Base Value + Rate per additional person).
The apartments and chalets have no limit of children that can be accommodated, however remember that 1 (one) child up to 6 years old is free, after the limit of 1 (one) child, we charge a fee per child staying. Children from 7 years old to 11 years old pay a minimal fee. Children over the age of 12 pay the additional person fee as an adult. Please remember that at the time of check-in the children's identification documents must be presented along with those of their legal guardians.
