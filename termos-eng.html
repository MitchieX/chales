General Policy

Check-In: 02:00 pm

Check-Out: 12:00 pm

Hosting Policy: The Check-In is done always from 2:00 pm local time and the Check-Out until 12:00 pm local time. At check-in it is necessary to present a valid identification document that is accepted throughout the national territory.

Check-Out held after 12:00 pm, may be charged by the establishment the value of the additional daily rate as a penalty without the possibility of enjoying the same by the guest for failure to comply with the standard of accommodation.

Explanation: All check-out should be done until 12:00 pm, because there is possibly another person who made the reservation for this day and by right can stay from 02:00 pm. Therefore, our cleaning time is in this interval from 12:00 pm to 2:00 pm, and for this reason it is not possible to extend the check-out time. The same way it happens with the check-in, it is not allowed to check-in before 2:00pm, because there is probably a guest in the room who can stay in his room until 12:00 pm, so we add the cleaning time and the apartment will be free from 02:00pm for check-in.

Sound policy and other sound devices: We do not allow any kind of sound inside the inn, whether it be automotive, speakers, radio or cell phones with loudspeakers that may pollute the local sound environment. Our focus is to make it possible for all guests to rest and relax, so as not to cause any discomfort or dissatisfaction.

Pet Policy: Pets are only allowed in the chalets, without the possibility of circulating in the other areas of the establishment without the proper accompaniment of the owner under the control of a collar or other command devices.

Cancellation Policy:The total amount of the reservations will be refunded in case of a cancellation informed 30 days before the check-in date.

In case of cancellation between 29 to 20 days from the check-in date, 50% of the total amount of the reservations will be charged.

In case of cancellation between 19 to 10 days from check-in date, 75% of the reservations will be charged.

Cancellations made between 9 days and 0 days before the check-in date will be charged the full amount of the reservations.

Exceptions excepted.

Space and Services: We offer to the Guests the Use of Our Services, Among them: WiFi; Pool; Pool Area; Barbecue; Sports Area (For Volleyball and Soccer); Playground; Security and Parking.
